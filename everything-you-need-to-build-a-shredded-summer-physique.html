Why the Summer Physique Matters

Summer physique isn't just about looking lean on the beach. It reflects discipline, health, and effort. The good news? You don't need perfect genetics. You need strategy and execution.

Start With Your BMR and Calories

Before doing anything, calculate your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate). It tells you how many calories you burn at rest. Subtract 300–500 calories from that to begin cutting fat safely.

Optimal Workout Plan for Summer Body

  • Lift 4–5 days/week. Prioritize compound lifts.
  • Include 20–30 minutes of cardio 3x/week.
  • Train abs 2–3 times/week for definition.
  • Stick to progressive overload and log your lifts.

Summer Diet Rules That Actually Work

  • High protein: 1–1.2g/lb of body weight
  • Moderate carbs timed around workouts
  • Low-moderate fats, mostly from whole foods
  • Hydrate heavily: 3–4 liters/day minimum
  • Limit processed foods and liquid calories

Top Fat-Burning Supplements (Optional)

  • Caffeine: boosts metabolism and energy
  • Green tea extract: may increase fat oxidation
  • Yohimbine: effective when taken fasted (not for beginners)
  • Whey isolate: hit protein goals without extra calories

How to Stay Consistent Until August

  • Track your weight weekly
  • Take progress photos every 2 weeks
  • Join a friend or community for accountability
  • Use mirrors and clothes fit instead of just scale
