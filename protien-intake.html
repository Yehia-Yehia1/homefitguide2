How Much Protein Do You Really Need?

By: Yehia Yehia | May 31, 2025

Why Protein Intake Matters

Your body uses protein to build and repair tissues. Whether you're trying to gain muscle, lose fat, or maintain your weight, getting the right amount of protein is critical.

Recommended Daily Protein Intake

Here's a general guide for protein needs:

  • Maintenance: 0.8 - 1.0g per kg of body weight
  • Fat Loss: 1.4 - 2.0g per kg
  • Muscle Gain: 1.6 - 2.2g per kg

To convert your weight to kilograms, divide your weight in pounds by 2.2
