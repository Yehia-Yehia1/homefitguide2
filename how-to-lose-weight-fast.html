How to Lose Weight Fast - Do's and Don'ts

By: Yehia Yehia | May 30, 2025

What You Should Do

  • Eat high-protein breakfasts to stay full longer
  • Track your calories daily
  • Exercise 4-5 times a week (focus on full body workouts)
  • Stay consistent with sleep and hydration
  • Use portion control to reduce overeating
  • Use a kitchen scale and food diary

What You Should Avoid

  • Skipping meals or relying on starvation
  • Over-reliance on supplements without changing your diet
  • Liquid-only diets or extreme detoxes
  • High-sugar "healthy" snacks
  • Late-night binges

  • Fitness Tracker - Monitor activity and sleep
