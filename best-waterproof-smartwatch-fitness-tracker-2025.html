Looking for a reliable waterproof fitness tracker or smartwatch? Here's a complete breakdown of the best wearables in 2025 based on battery life, accuracy, design, and water resistance. Ideal for swimmers, runners, and everyday users.

Why Waterproof Fitness Trackers Matter

Waterproof fitness trackers allow you to monitor health metrics during swimming, showers, and rainy workouts. Devices with at least a 5 ATM rating can be submerged up to 50 meters, making them ideal for active lifestyles.

How to Choose the Right Device

  • Water Resistance: Look for 5 ATM or IP68 minimum
  • Battery Life: More than 5 days is ideal
  • Features: Heart rate, SpO2, GPS, ECG, sleep tracking
  • Compatibility: Ensure sync with iOS or Android
  • Budget: Don’t overspend on unnecessary features

FAQs

Can I wear these while swimming daily?

Yes. Devices with 5 ATM are built for swimming and water sports.

Do smartwatches track heart rate underwater?

Most premium trackers continue monitoring heart rate under water.

Is ECG available in budget models?

ECG is mostly found in mid to high-end models like Fitbit Charge 6 and Galaxy Watch 6.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Choosing fitness bands with no IP or ATM rating
  • Overlooking battery life and daily charging needs
  • Not checking compatibility with your phone OS
