Why Calcium Matters

Calcium is essential for strong bones and preventing osteoporosis. Adults typically need between 1,000 and 1,200 mg of calcium daily, depending on age and gender. Use this calculator to track your intake and make sure you're getting enough!

Tips to Boost Your Calcium Intake

  • Eat more leafy greens, nuts, and dairy or fortified alternatives.
  • Consider supplements if you can't get enough from food.
  • Pair calcium-rich foods with vitamin D sources for better absorption.
